About Us

Welcome to Simple Tech Services

Simple Tech Services is a 100% Papua New Guinea–owned business, proudly founded in 2019 by Managing Director Mr. Pau Ukuma. With a passion for technology and a self-taught skillset developed through online platforms like YouTube, Mr. Ukuma began by repairing computers and gradually expanded to software installations and selling refurbished devices.

His dedication and growing customer demand quickly turned his side hustle into a trusted local service. In 2020, he officially registered Simple Tech Services with the Investment Promotion Authority (IPA), marking the beginning of a fully established and growing business..

After officially registering Simple Tech Services, the business expanded quickly, adding new services such as photography, videography, ID card production, and basic computer skills training.
Through these initiatives, Simple Tech Services had the privilege of helping many young people—as well as some older members of the community—in Popondetta, where STS operated for about a year. Explore more about our journey and the services we now offer as you browse through our website.

Managing Director Message

Simple TECH Services

Like many businesses—both big and small—Simple Tech Services was significantly impacted by the COVID-19 pandemic. Operations were put on hold, and the break extended even beyond the pandemic. During this time, Simple Tech Services relocated from Popondetta to Port Moresby. Unfortunately, the business name registration expired during the transition, and the renewal process took longer than expected. Despite these challenges, Simple Tech Services remained committed to rebuilding and returning stronger.

As the Managing Director of Simple Tech Services, I am proud to say that we are making a strong comeback — this time with renewed energy, expanded services, and an even greater commitment to making technology simple and accessible for everyone.

Typing, Printing & Binding

We offer fast and accurate typing services, along with high-quality printing for documents, assignments, forms, letters, and web content. We also provide binding, laminating, and more — all in one convenient place..

Computer Repair & Software Installation

We provide fast and reliable computer repair services to fix hardware and software issues. From screen replacements and hard drive upgrades to virus removal and performance tuning, we’ve got you covered. We also handle software installation, including operating systems, Microsoft Office, antivirus, and other essential applications — ensuring your computer is fully functional and secure..

Social Media Setup & Management

We help individuals and businesses establish a strong online presence by setting up and managing social media accounts such as Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, and more. Our service includes profile creation, branding, page optimization, regular content posting, and audience engagement — helping you grow your reach and connect with customers effectively..

Basic Computer Skills & Network Training

We provide training that equips participants with the essential knowledge to confidently use computers and understand basic networking concepts.
The Training covers practical computer skills such as typing, file management, using common software (e.g. Word, Excel), and browsing the internet. The networking component introduces how devices connect, basic troubleshooting, and concepts like LAN and WAN.
